1. Q - I am not able to login with my SFCS account.

A. My password is incorrect

B. I am not receiving the OTP

Answer - Please email to ITHELPDESK@ST-FRANCIS.ORG. Attach the screenshot of the error message and we will get back to you.


2. Q - I am able to login but I am getting white screen and not able to view CMS module.

Answer-1. Clear the browser cache

i. Steps - Click More tools Clear browsing data.

ii. at the top, choose a time range. To delete everything, select All time.

iii. Next to "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files," check the boxes.

iv. Click Clear data.

v. Now open a new browser and try.
