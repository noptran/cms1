STEPS OF ACTION

When you observe or are part of a Critical/ Significant/Unusual Incident, proceed through the following steps:

1. Take immediate action as needed (i.e. safety, emergency procedures, first aid)

2. Decide if emergent assistance from anyone is needed

3. Contact Appropriate People (Parents, Administration, Police, Fire Department, Director of Clinical, On-Call Staff, Nurse)

4. Complete the Critical/Significant/Unusual Incident report in CMS within 24 hours

5. If not entered into CMS, send Critical/Significant/Unusual Incident Form to SFM Risk Management by fax to 785-825-0685, email zzRiskManagement@st-francis.org, or mail directly to SFM Risk Management at 509 E Elm, Salina, KS 67401

6. Notify administration if not already informed

7. Allegations of abuse and neglect must be reported immediately

8. Follow instructions below per Program

DEFINITIONS: CI = Critical Incident SI=Significant Incident
