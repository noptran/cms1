The Placement Provider Agrees To:

1.Work cooperatively with SFM in providing care for the child that meets his/her physical, emotional, spiritual and educational needs. Actively participate in the development and implementation of the child’s care plan, including scheduling of parental and sibling visitation

2.I have read, signed, and agree to follow the SFM Confidentiality Policy.

3.Obtain needed medical and dental care for the child in a timely manner.

4.Placement Provider agrees to work with SFM to transport the child to and from medical, mental health, and other appointments relating to the child.

5.Notify SFM staff immediately of any serious illness or injury, runaway, or major incident or event of the child.

6.Obtain prior permission from SFM before moving to a new address or taking the child out of the State of Kansas.

7.Refrain from any and all disciplinary action administered in a way that could cause the child to suffer physical or emotional harm. I have read, signed, and agree to follow the agency Discipline Policy.

8.Maintain strict confidentiality in sharing any information about the child or his/her birth family

9.Maintain/preserve all personal possessions of the child and relinquish said possessions upon termination of the placement

10.Placement Provider agrees to give at least a 14-day notice prior to requesting the removal of the child from the home.

11.Obtain up-to-date records on the child

12.SFM terminating this placement before the agreement expiration date when this child is deemed discharged by SFM at no cost to SFM.

13.SFM terminating this agreement upon the contractor changing the level of care at no cost to SFM

14.Cooperate with SFM staff completing assessments, updates, safety plans, and stability staffing’s as needed to address stability and safety concerns.

15.Obtain permission prior to any adult who may reside in the home who does not have current KBI and Canis backgrounds.

16.Maintain the home to the standard of the safety walkthrough for the duration of placement.

SFM Agrees To:

1.Make payment as stated above for the child placed in the Placement Provider’s home, to provide for the needs of said child. A payment will be issued by the 15th of every month. Payment shall start the first day of placement and end on the last full day of placement in the home; discharge date is an unpaid day.

2.Obtain and share prior medical, dental and other pertinent information about the child, such as psychosocial, psychological and educational information.

3.The Placement Provider and other team members developing a Case Plan (relative placements only).

4.Provide 24 hour-a-day on-call accessibility to SFM staff for emergencies. (1-888-SFA-HOPE)

5.Provide necessary forms for completion of required documentation.
