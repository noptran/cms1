WAIVER OF 30-DAY NOTICE OF PLANNED MOVE GUIDE

Purpose

As provided for in KSA 38-2258, a letter is to be completed to formally give 30 days notice of a planned move to the child(ren)’s Current Placement, Parents for whom an address is known, the Child if age 12 and over, the GAL, the County Attorney, the Parents’ Attorney(s), the SRS Worker and anyone recognized by the court as an interested party. The notice must be given if the child has been residing in their current placement for six (6) months or longer or the child has been residing in the home of their parent or relative regardless of the length of time. The Waiver of 30-Day Notice of Planned Move form is to be included with the letter. The move may take place prior to the expiration of the 30 days if all parties notified agree in writing to the move.

Requirements

This form should be included with the 30-Day Notice of Planned Move Letter for the following recipients:

Other Interested Parties

This form is used to waive the right to a 30-day notice of such a move. If all parties notified agree in writing to the move, the move may take place prior to the expiration of the 30 days.
